02 Permission Explainer

Explain inbox access before asking for it.

This screen should make Gmail access feel explicit, limited, and useful. The user should understand value before seeing the OAuth consent prompt.

Why Tasktify needs access

Tasktify reads your connected inbox to detect requests, implied commitments, and follow-ups that may need action.

What we read

  • Email and message content needed to detect tasks
  • Sender, timestamp, and thread context
  • Follow-up patterns like unanswered requests

What we never do

  • Send replies on your behalf
  • Create tasks without review
  • Share your message content with other users

Your control

You can review suggestions before saving, revisit permissions later, and disconnect channels in settings.
